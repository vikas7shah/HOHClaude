Smart Pantry Tracking

Scan receipts or tell HOH what you bought. It tracks what's in your fridge, what's expiring, and suggests meals to use things up before they go bad.

Shared Ownership

Assign meal owners, set personal constraints ("no cooking Thursdays"), and give everyone visibility. No more one person carrying the mental load alone.

Smart Grocery Lists

Auto-generated from your meal plan, minus what you already have. Organized by store section. Shareable with anyone doing the shopping.

Dietary Intelligence

Keto for you, carbs for the kids, dairy-free for Emma. HOH juggles multiple diets in one household without you having to think about it.

Daily Briefings

Everyone gets a morning text: "Tonight's dinner: Tacos. Jake is cooking. Start at 5:30." No more asking "what's for dinner?" 47 times.

1-Tap Swaps

Life happens. Say "I'm exhausted" and HOH swaps tonight's meal for a 15-min backup. The whole family gets notified automatically.

Voice-Powered

Hands full? Just talk. "Add milk to the pantry." "Emma doesn't like broccoli." "We're out of pasta." HOH listens and updates instantly.

Snap & Add

Take a photo of your grocery receipt or open fridge. HOH's AI identifies everything and updates your pantry automatically. No manual entry needed.
